About ZBCW

The ZBCW is a business Trust founded by eminent business companies in Zimbabwe 20 years ago. These include : Delta Beverages ,Zimplants, Dairibord, OLD MUTUAL ,Unilever, Standard Chartered Bank, OK zimbabwe, Starafrica and Anglo America among others. Its goal is promoting culture of Wellness in private sector business.

ZBCW is a holistic wellness cohort founded as a strategic support system for businesses and the nation at large. Its focus is channelled into six pillars.
