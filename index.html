About ZBCW

The ZBCW is a trust that provides strategic support to businesses to manage HIV and Wellness in a manner that guarantees their viability, growth and sustainability of business. Comprehensive HIV and Wellness programs will ensure the elimination of the impact of HIV on business and enhance employee productivity by managing their total wellness.

The ZBCW views the development and implementation if comprehensive wellness programs as a means of managing the company human assets thereby eliminating the business risk of HIV and lack of wellness.
